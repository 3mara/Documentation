\section{Uuser Interface Components}
\subsection{Home Screen}
The Home screen of articles published up-to-date, each article appears in a three column layout similar to the magazine style to facilitate the user browsing.
In each Article there exits two buttons for taking user feedback for the current article used in building the user’s profile.
\subsection{History}
This part is responsible for showing the articles that have been viewed by a certain users and their feedback on these articles showed as a score for each article.
\subsection{Recommendation View}
Finally this component has the role of displaying the top $N$ recommended articles based on user activity of “Like” or “Dislike” the browsed articles.
